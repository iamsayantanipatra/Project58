My favourite hobby is programming!

Programming


Programming is a sequence of instructions written in a proper language through which the computer can understand and solve the problem given to it. It is the method by which the whole computing process is directed and controlled. Preparing a program for the computer is known as “programming”.

I love coding. Ever since I bought my first computer (a HP Laptop 17-by4097nr), I’ve been fascinated by computer programming. For many months, it has been almost it's going to be 1 year, learning and doing coding. I never thought of why I enjoyed it so much – I just knew I did, Now, after experiencing several months, doing coding. I now know that why I love coding!


I love coding because- it really excites me, it is fun to do. It really excites me whenever I come across a problem, I get curious about solving it. I am a person who loves to solve puzzles, questions involving logic and thinking ability.



Some reasons why I love coding because-

1 .It helps me automate some of my daily and simple tasks. If I have to do something repeatedly, I will prefer to code it and then run that code again and again.

2. It is a really interesting and fun activity to do. Imagine creating a solution to a problem that can be used by people to make their work easy.

3. I am a person who loves puzzles and problems involving logic. I can be seen playing Arcade or Puzzle category games in my free time.

4. You can create something that can be extremely useful to people that can make their life and work easier.

5. I love that feeling you get when your solution gets an AC in some contest.
